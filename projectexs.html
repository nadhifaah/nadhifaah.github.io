Forecasting Course

Final Project

To meet the needs of electrical energy, Spain uses fossil and non-fossil materials. Based on data from Red Eléctrica de Espaa, the operator of the Spanish power grid, 18% of Spain's gross electricity generation came from wind energy, 14% from hydropower, 5% from solar and 2% from other renewable sources in 2016. depending on nature, so it is necessary to forecast for renewable energy that can be produced and then also predict the load from the community, so that it can be obtained how much energy must be produced by fossil generators. The method that will be used is ARIMA and Neural network, where the dataset from the dataset obtained will then be split into 80% training data and 20% test data, then for the Neural Network the MLP (Multi Layer Perceptron) type is selected. Then the results obtained are that the MAPE for the model carried is 2.31%, which is still below the TSO which is 1.1%. This one can still be improved with the data engineering feature

GIGIH 2.0 2022

Business Desicion Making: Cookie Cats

Teamwork assessment with me as the leader. Cookie Cats is a hugely popular mobile puzzle game developed by Tactile Entertainment. It's a classic "connect three" style puzzle game where the player must connect tiles of the same color in order to clear the board and win the level. It also features singing cats. As players progress through the game they will encounter gates that force them to wait some time before they can progress or make an in-app purchase. In this project, we will analyze the result of an experiment where the first gate in Cookie Cats was moved from level 30 to level 40. In particular, we will analyze the impact on player retention and game rounds.
